product Description

The Astrid Cafe Chair and Astrid Club Chair combine modern elegance with ergonomic comfort, making them ideal for both commercial and residential settings. Designed with clean lines and a contemporary silhouette, these chairs elevate any space with their minimalist charm.


Astrid Cafe Chair:

Perfect for cafes, restaurants, and dining spaces, this lightweight yet sturdy chair features a curved backrest for comfortable support and a durable frame built for daily use. Available in a variety of finishes and upholstery options to complement any décor


Astrid Club Chair:

A luxurious addition to lounges, lobbies, or living areas, the club chair version offers a wider seat, plush cushioning, and a more relaxed seating posture. Upholstered in high-quality fabric or leather, the Astrid Club Chair balances style with indulgent comfort.


    Featured

  • Sleek modern design with Scandinavian influence
  • Durable metal or wooden frame (customizable options available)
  • Upholstery options: high-resilience fabric, faux leather, or genuine leather
  • Available in a range of colors and finishes
  • Suitable for commercial and residential use
  • Optional armrests (for Club Chair)
