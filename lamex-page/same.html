Product Description

Sure is a family of chairs that includes high chair, café chair. Sure is a designed and collaborative chair, which can be used for works and catering area of a workplace. Sure is covered by quality fabric, giving you a soft and comfortable texture. The rich colorways and the slim base are designed to create a strong sense of beauty.


    Featured

  • Versatile Range: Includes high chair and café chair formats for different functional areas.
  • Premium Upholstery: Soft, durable fabric in a variety of contemporary tones.
  • Minimalist Silhouette: Slim, powder-coated metal base for a modern aesthetic.
  • Ergonomic Comfort: Contoured seat and backrest designed for daily use.
  • Rich Colorways: Customizable hues to complement any design scheme.

    Specifications

  • Frame Material: Powder-coated steel
  • Seat Material: High-density foam with fabric upholstery
  • Backrest: Slightly curved for lumbar support
  • Available Heights: Café chair (standard), High chair (bar/counter)
  • Color Options: Charcoal Grey, Midnight Blue, Terracotta Red, Olive Green, Sand Beige
  • Weight Capacity: Up to 120 kg (265 lbs)
