product Description

Sure is a family of chairs that includes high chair, café chair. Sure is a designed and collaborative chair, which can be used for works and catering area of a workplace. Sure is covered by quality fabric, giving you a soft and comfortable texture. The rich colorways and the slim base are designed to create a strong sense of beauty.


    Specifications

  • Frame Material: Powder-coated steel or lightweight aluminum
  • Upholstery: Premium-grade fabric with soft, tactile finish
  • Color Options: Multiple rich colorways available
  • Base Type: Slim sled or four-leg base (depending on model)
  • Optional Features: Armrests, footrest (for high chair), felt glides

    Dimensions

    For Café Chair:

  • Height: 800 mm
  • Seat Height: 460 mm
  • Width: 520 mm
  • Depth: 550 mm

    For High Chair:

  • Height: 1050 mm
  • Seat Height: 750 mm
  • Width: 520 mm
  • Depth: 550 mm
